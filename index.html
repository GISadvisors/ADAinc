Our Project

The Municipality of Port Hope currently houses thousands of water and wastewater service records in physical form. Managing physical documents is costly, inefficient and makes it difficult to track inventory. To improve their asset management practices and be able to locate utility infrastructure in the field, the Municipality desired to have their service lines and valves mapped as accurately as possible.

Over the course of the project, three solutions to map data were explored: automation to plot points, manually mapping points, and utilizing a mobile data collection application. The amount of time to use and accuracy were compared for each solution and Port Hope's existing geodatabases were updated using the results.
